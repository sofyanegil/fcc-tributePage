Prof. Dr. Ing. H. Bacharuddin Jusuf Habibie, FREng

Mr. Crack from Indonesia invent The Crack Theory in Airplane

Here's a time line of Mr. Habibie life:

  • 1936 - Born in Parepare
  • 1964 - Graduated from high School in Bandung
  • 1960 - Achive Diploma Degree from Technische Hochschule, Germany
  • 1962 - Married with Mrs. Ainun Besari Habibie
  • 1965 - Achive Doctor Ingenieur from rhenisch wesfalische Technische Hochschule, Germany
  • 1965 - Work in Messerschmitt-Bölkow-Blohm as a Head of Research and Development in Airplane Craft Structure
  • 1967 - Promoted to honorary professor from Bandung Techonology Institute
  • 1969 - Became a Head Division Method and Technology for analytical commercial and military aircraft Industry
  • 1974 - Asked by President Soeharto to return to the country to help build advanced industries
  • 1976 - Became a government adviser and chief of a new aerospace company
  • 1978 - Became research minister and head of the Agency for Technology Evaluation and Application
  • 1998 - Became a 3rd President of Indonesia
  • 2000 - Established the Habibier Center, a political research institute
  • 2019 - Passed away in Hospital Army Center Gatot Soebroto
